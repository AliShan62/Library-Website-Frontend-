INTRODUCTION

Superior Information Resource Center (SIRC) libraries are designed and operated under the mission and objective of the Superior University which lead curators to facilitate and fulfill the print and digital needs of the students, staff and faculty. SIRC collect and promote English and Urdu language academic and general research materials which help users in their undergraduate and graduate studies.

The Superior University SIRC’s are consist of Main Campus, Gold campus and Law Campus libraries. The main campus SIRC covers Medical, Dentistry, Physiotherapy, Allied Health Science, Engineering, Aviation, and Art and Design resources. In contrast, Gold Campus Covers Computer Science and Law Campus covers Law-related digital and print resources.

The curators of SIRC are available to deliver training to students, faculty and staff on the effective use of SIRC Online Public Access Catalogue (OPAC), digital and electronic resources and bibliometric research applications.
