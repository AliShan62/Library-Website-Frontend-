About Us

Main University Campus


The University of Education, Lahore was established on September 02, 2002 with the vision to produce dynamic leaders and practitioners in teaching, research and management having content excellence and pedagogical competence. University has highly prestigious governance system which is open and transparent for the stakeholders. All the public affairs and managing of public resources is made efficiently and prudently by the Leadership of the Institution. The current leadership has taken several steps to revolutionize the old traditional system and develop online and speedy system with transparency and integrity.

Lower mall Campus


On September 10, 2002, the Govt. of the Punjab, through an ordinance, established University of Education and Govt. College of Education was declared a campus of the newly established university. Since then, the Lower Mall Campus has been rendering commendable services to the cause of education in Pakistan. The Campus offers various programs including M.Ed., MA Education, MPhil Education, PhD Education, MPhil Urdu, PhD Urdu, BBA(Hons), MBA 3.5 Years, MBA 1.5 Year, MSc Economics and BS English. In the academic year 2013-14, two new courses B.Ed. Hons (4 years) and MA Special Education are being launched. The Lower Mall Campus has been the center of research activity in the field of teacher training and education.
